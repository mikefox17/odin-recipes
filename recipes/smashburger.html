Ingredients

  • Ground beef (80/20 blend), about 2 oz per patty
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Soft burger buns (e.g., potato or brioche)
  • American cheese slices
  • Butter or oil for the griddle
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Shredded lettuce (optional)
  • Sliced tomato (optional)
  • Diced or sliced onions (optional)
  • Smash sauce or preferred burger sauce (optional)

Recipe

  1. Preheat a cast iron skillet or griddle over medium-high heat until very hot.
  2. Divide ground beef into 2 oz balls (do not season yet).
  3. Place a beef ball on the hot surface and immediately smash it flat using a heavy spatula or burger press.
  4. Season the smashed patty with salt and pepper.
  5. Cook for about 1–2 minutes until the edges are crispy and browned.
  6. Flip the patty and immediately add a slice of American cheese.
  7. Cook for another 1–2 minutes until cheese is melted and patty is cooked through.
  8. Toast the burger buns lightly on the skillet or griddle.
  9. Assemble the burger: place patty on bun, add pickles, onions, lettuce, tomato, and sauce as desired.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy!
