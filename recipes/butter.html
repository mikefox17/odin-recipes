Compound Butter

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice (optional)
  • ½ teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper

Recipe

  1. Let the butter sit at room temperature until softened.
  2. Mince the garlic cloves finely or use a garlic press.
  3. Chop the fresh parsley finely.
  4. In a bowl, combine the softened butter, minced garlic, chopped parsley, lemon juice (if using), salt, and black pepper.
  5. Mix thoroughly with a fork or spatula until all ingredients are well incorporated.
  6. Transfer the mixture onto a piece of parchment or plastic wrap and shape into a log.
  7. Wrap tightly and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to firm up.
  8. Slice and use as needed on bread, steaks, vegetables, or seafood.
