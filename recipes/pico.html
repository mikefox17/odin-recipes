Ingredients

  • Diced Tomato
  • White Onion
  • Serrano Pepper
  • Lime
  • Cilantro
  • Salt and pepper

Recipe

  1. Dice 3 medium ripe tomatoes.
  2. Finely chop 1 small white onion.
  3. Seed and mince 1–2 serrano peppers (adjust to taste).
  4. Chop a handful of fresh cilantro leaves.
  5. Juice 1 lime and add to the mixture.
  6. Add salt to taste (about ½ teaspoon).
  7. Mix all ingredients in a bowl until well combined.
  8. Let sit for 15–30 minutes to allow flavors to meld.
  9. Serve with tortilla chips or as a topping for tacos, grilled meats, or salads.
